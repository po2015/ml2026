## 3. Personal Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary for the stated purpose.

**Information you provide directly:**

**Information collected automatically:**

**Information we do not collect:**
We do not collect sensitive personal information as defined under PIPL (including biometrics, religious belief, health data, or financial account credentials) unless you explicitly provide it as part of a project and give separate consent.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

All personal data collected from visitors and clients is stored on servers located **within the People's Republic of China**, operated by certified cloud infrastructure providers that comply with the Cybersecurity Law and relevant national standards (GB/T 22239).

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures including:
- Encrypted transmission (TLS 1.2 or higher)
- Access controls and least-privilege permissions
- Regular security assessments

---

## 6. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Where we need to transfer your personal information outside the PRC (for example, to deliver a localization project involving overseas counterparts), we will:

1. Conduct a **security assessment** in accordance with the Measures for Security Assessment of Cross-border Data Transfer (数据出境安全评估办法);
2. Enter into a **standard contract** approved by the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC); or
3. Obtain your explicit **separate consent** for the transfer.

We will not transfer personal information to jurisdictions that do not provide an equivalent level of protection without your prior consent.

## 7. Your Rights Under PIPL and GDPR

As a data subject you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of your personal information we hold
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion** — request erasure of your data, subject to legal retention obligations
- **Restriction / Objection** — object to or restrict certain processing activities
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** — at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
- **Explanation of automated decisions** — we currently do not make solely automated decisions with legal effects

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@medialocalize.com. We will respond within **15 working days** as required under PIPL.
