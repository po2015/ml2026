How It Works

From brief to live in 2–3 weeks

A structured sprint — not an open-ended agency retainer.

1
Discovery & Brief Day 1
One call to capture your brand, audience, competitors, and goals. We feed the brief directly into Claude Code to generate an architecture proposal — component tree, page structure, and recommended stack — for your review before a single line of code is written.
2
Design & AI Content Days 2–5
Figma wireframes and high-fidelity mockups are reviewed with you before build. Claude writes the first draft of all copy — product pages, about, SEO meta — tuned to your keyword targets. A human editor reviews every word before it ships.
3
AI-Assisted Build Days 5–12
Developers build with GitHub Copilot active and Codex generating API integrations and form logic. Every commit triggers GitHub Actions: ESLint, type-checking, Lighthouse audit, and automatic preview deployment so you can review in real-time.
4
Review & Launch Days 13–16
You review the staging URL and request revisions. On approval, vercel --prod pushes to all edge nodes. We submit your sitemap to Google Search Console, configure GA4, and activate the AI chatbot with your product data.
5
Ongoing Support Monthly
Monthly content updates, SEO reporting, Lighthouse re-audits, dependency security patches, and AI chatbot retraining as your product line evolves. You own the repo on GitHub — full source access, no lock-in.
