Scope of Work

Every element localized

E-learning localization is more than translating text. We adapt every layer of your course for the target learner.

  • UI text, menus, buttons, and navigation labels
  • Slide text, captions, and on-screen annotations
  • Audio narration — AI or human voice replacement
  • Embedded video with subtitles or full dubbing
  • Quiz questions, answer options, and feedback messages
  • Branching scenario dialogue and decision prompts
  • Certificate and completion page text
  • Culturally inappropriate imagery flagged and replaced
  • RTL layout adaptation (Arabic, Hebrew)
  • CJK typography and character spacing (Chinese, Japanese, Korean)

Process

From source course to global learners

1
Content Audit
We analyse your SCORM package or source files to identify all translatable text, audio tracks, video elements, and images requiring cultural adaptation. You receive a fixed word count and media duration.
2
Translation & Script Adaptation
Subject-matter linguists translate all text and adapt scripts for audio re-recording. Scripts are timed to match the original narration pacing so animations stay in sync.
3
Multimedia Production
Audio narration is recorded with AI or human voices and synced to course animations. Video content receives subtitles or full dubbing. On-screen text graphics are rebuilt in the target language.
4
Course Rebuild & QA
The localized content is integrated back into the authoring tool. A separate QA reviewer plays through the entire course, checking navigation, quiz logic, branching, audio sync, and all text for accuracy.
5
LMS Testing & Delivery
The published SCORM/xAPI package is tested in SCORM Cloud and, if required, in your specific LMS environment. We confirm completion tracking, pass/fail thresholds, and certificate generation work correctly before final delivery.
